MobileX was founded in 2018 by serial entrepreneur Jas Chahal based on the vision "innovation for everyone". We believe that high-quality products built with cutting-edge technology should be made accessible to everyone. We create remarkable hardware, software, and Internet services for and with the help of our fans. We incorporate their feedback into our product range, which currently includes smartphones and other accessories like smartwatches, Truely Wirerless Buds and much more. With presence in over 30 countries and regions, MobileX is expanding its footprint across the world to become a global brand.
