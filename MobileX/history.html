In 2017, our company was a software company creating custom ROM's based on Google's Android. In beginning of 2018, we started creating our own hardware at affordable price. By the end of 2018, We announced our first smartphone, PhoneX. The PhoneX is a top spec phone with a great price way ahead of its time, and this has always remained our company philosophy from day one. We have launched many other devices such as smartwatches and headphones and we are even looking to launch new devices in upcoming months like SmartTv, Tablets, fitness bands and much more.



Our jouney....

This whole journey began 2 years back when group of five friends came with an idea of starting a smartphone company that provides high-quality smartphones with flagship specifications at an affordable price. The smartphones which had the latest technology, powerful processors and such flagship specification which were only considered a rich man's legacy as per the market trend then. Providing flagship specifications at a budget price was the biggest challenge. With software developers, marketing experts and with accurate technical guidance our team of five friends was ready to begin our dream. Though there was a long way to go. There were challenges but hard work is something that always pays off. We had the idea but to shape it and land in the right direction was not easy. For students, it was hard to invest in such a business. After a year of struggle, an investor was ready to invest in our business and then there was no looking back. We came up with the product Phone X which had a powerful processor, high-resolution camera, reduced thickness and other latest features at an affordable price. Even though It was our first smartphone, it was a big success as many customers enjoyed our product and it's pricing. This made us realize our capabilities, endurance, passion and enthusiasm brought us where Mobile X stands today.
